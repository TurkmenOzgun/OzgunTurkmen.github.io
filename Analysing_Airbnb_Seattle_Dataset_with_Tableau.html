Analysing Airbnb Seattle Dataset with Tableau

Final dashboard link: https://public.tableau.com/views/AirbnbSeattleProject_16490244231700/Dashboard1

In this project, I will use Tableau to visualize Seattle Airbnb Data.

Seattle Airbnb data is the most upvoted Airbnb dataset in Kaggle.

Data includes;

  • Listings, including full descriptions and average review score

  • Reviews, including a unique id for each reviewer and detailed comments

  • Calendar, including listing id and the price and availability for that day

Problem: Our business partner wants to hire out his homes on the Airbnb website and he asked our help to analyse Airbnb data to see to make the best business decisions.

Downloaded the data from the Kaggle website.

https://www.kaggle.com/datasets/airbnb/seattle



Used these 3 documents and combined them and uploaded them to Tableau Public program.
