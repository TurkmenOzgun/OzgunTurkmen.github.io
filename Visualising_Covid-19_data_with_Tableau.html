Visualising Covid-19 data with Tableau

In this project, I am going to analyse Covid-19 data using SQL codes. We are going to see cases, tests and vaccination numbers up to countries. This project does not have visualisation. I am going to visualise these results on another project.

In this project, I will visualize the International Covid-19 data with Tableau. The data is from my another project - SQL Covid.

You can find the final dashboard from this project: https://public.tableau.com/app/profile/ozgun.turkmen/viz/Covid-19-Project/Dashboard1

Note: This Covid-data is from https://ourworldindata.org/covid-deaths.

Data is between February 15, 2020, and February 22, 2022.

  1. Total Covid-19 cases by countries:

The United States, India and Brazil are the countries which have the most Covid-19 cases.


  1. Covid-19 cases per population

When we look at cases up to their population, we can see that small European countries have the biggest cases/population numbers. 

Top 3 countries:

Faroe Islands: 65.33

Andorra: 48.89

Denmark: 45:37


3- Top 10 countries by death per population:

The death rate per population is very high in eastern European countries. The top is Bulgaria with a 0.51 rate.


4- Vaccinated people rate  per population

In the United Arab Emirates, almost everybody is vaccinated at least 1 time. Portugal, Cuba and Chile are following them.


5- Correlation between vaccination and cases

When we check the 2 tables, we don’t see any direct correlation between vaccination and cases. All these countries are high vaccinated countries. But they have different volumes of cases per population rate.


6- Vaccination time series USA vs Germany

I wanted to compare between USA and Germany regarding daily vaccination numbers.



It is expected that the USA have more vaccination because of their larger population. 


It is visible that the USA started to vaccination process earlier and faster than Germany. But the second peak point is more or less at the same time. (around December of 2021)
